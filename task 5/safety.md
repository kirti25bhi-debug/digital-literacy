Stay Safe Online
General Safety
 Use strong, unique passwords for each account
 Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
 Avoid clicking on suspicious links or unknown attachments
 Verify website URLs before entering personal information
 Do not share OTPs or passwords with anyone
 Keep your devices and apps updated regularly
UPI / Financial Safety
 Never approve unknown payment requests on UPI apps
 Double-check receiver details before sending money
 Avoid scanning random QR codes from unknown sources
 Do not share bank details on calls or messages
Reporting Cybercrime
 Report incidents at National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
 Call Helpline: 1930 for immediate financial fraud assistance
